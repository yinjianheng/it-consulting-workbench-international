# Phase 0: Business Development & Contracting

## Phase Objective
The full process from first contact with a potential client to signing the project contract.

## Complete Workflow
```
Initial Client Meeting → Requirements Clarification & Problem Diagnosis → Project Proposal → SOW Contract & Negotiation → Signing
```

## Prerequisites
- Client proactively reaches out or opportunity identified through business channels
- General understanding of the client's industry and enterprise scale

## Phase Deliverables
| Deliverable | Description | Audience |
|--------|------|--------|
| Client Meeting Minutes | Structured record of initial/follow-up communications | Internal Team |
| Problem Diagnosis Summary | Problem Statement + Preliminary Hypotheses | Internal + Client |
| Project Proposal | Formal business proposal | Client Decision-Makers |
| Statement of Work (SOW) | Contract appendix defining scope/deliverables/timeline/fees | Both Signing Parties |

## Key Success Factors
- **Build trust within the first 3 interactions**: Use structured questioning to demonstrate professionalism — never talk methodology in the abstract
- **Speed**: Deliver meeting minutes and problem diagnosis summary within 48 hours of the initial meeting
- **Don't rush to quote**: Never discuss pricing before fully understanding the requirements
- **Don't overpromise**: Clearly state what you can and cannot do — maintain clear professional boundaries
