## VII. Resistance Management Plan

### 7.1 Anticipated Resistance

| Possible Resistance | Manifestation | Root Cause | Response |
|--------------|------|------|------|
| "This system definitely won't work well" | Skipping training / passive usage | Past failed system go-live experiences | Early demo + pilot proof + success stories |
| "Will my job be replaced?" | Anxiety / resistance / spreading negativity | Fear of AI/automation replacing humans | Clarify that change ≠ layoffs, new skills = new value |
| "Too busy, no time to learn" | Delaying training / making excuses | Change is outside KPIs, no priority | Get leadership to include change adoption in performance metrics |

### 7.2 Resistance Escalation Path
```
Level 1: Individual Resistance → Change Champion 1-on-1 conversation
Level 2: Small Group Resistance → Change Lead + Department Leader conversation
Level 3: Department-Level Resistance → Sponsor intervention + plan adjustment
Level 4: Systemic Resistance → Pause, reassess change strategy
```
