## Scoring Criteria (1-5 Scale)

| Score | Level | Description |
|------|------|------|
| 5 | Exceptional | Significantly exceeds requirements, industry-leading, delivers surprises |
| 4 | Good | Fully meets requirements, partially exceeds |
| 3 | Adequate | Basically meets requirements, no significant weaknesses |
| 2 | Insufficient | Has gaps, requires significant customization or compromise |
| 1 | Severely Deficient | Core requirements cannot be met |

**5 Must-Ask Questions for Reference Calls:**
1. "If you could choose again, would you still choose them? Why?"
2. "What surprised you most during the project implementation? (Positive and negative)"
3. "How much did the actual post-go-live TCO differ from your original estimate?"
4. "Is the vendor's support response truly as stated in the contract?"
5. "What do you wish you had written into the contract but now realize was missing?"
