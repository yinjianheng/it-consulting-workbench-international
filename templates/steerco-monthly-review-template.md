## SteerCo Presentation Tips

**Before the Meeting:**
- [ ] Send materials 2 days in advance so members can review
- [ ] Pre-brief Sponsor separately (do not "ambush" the Sponsor at SteerCo)
- [ ] Prepare Top 5 possible questions that may be asked

**During the Meeting:**
- [ ] Present status and conclusions in the first minute
- [ ] Spend no more than 2 minutes per slide
- [ ] Be honest about risks/issues — don't sugarcoat
- [ ] Decision requests must be specific and actionable (not just "please be supportive")

**After the Meeting:**
- [ ] Send meeting minutes within 24 hours
- [ ] Every decision has a clear follow-up action
